Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Who We Are

CrisisConnect is an independent humanitarian information platform founded in Nigeria. We connect people with verified aid organizations and crisis information. We do not process payments, hold funds, or act as a financial intermediary of any kind.

What Data We Collect

CrisisConnect does not collect personal data on a server. All data generated during your visit — such as page views, link clicks, and session information — is stored locally in your own browser using localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

If you submit the contact form, your message is also stored locally in your browser. To actually send us a message, please use the email or phone number listed on the Contact page directly.

Cookies & Tracking

We do not use cookies. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any external tracking service. The analytics visible in the admin panel are entirely local to the device being used.

Third-Party Links

CrisisConnect links directly to external NGO websites including UNHCR, WFP, MSF, UNICEF, IRC, CARE, Save the Children, and ICRC. When you click these links, you leave CrisisConnect and are subject to the privacy policies of those organizations. We are not responsible for the content or practices of external sites.

Maps

Our resource map uses OpenStreetMap tiles via Leaflet.js. OpenStreetMap's own privacy policy applies to map tile requests. No personally identifiable information is shared.

Children's Privacy

CrisisConnect does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The platform is designed to be accessible to all ages for humanitarian information purposes only.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the platform grows. Changes will be reflected with an updated date at the top of this page.
